The goal of this website is to create a portal through which the voting populous is able to research candidates running for government office. We understand we're not the first, however it is our goal to provide information not only on Senators and Congressional office. We want to let you compare local candidates just as easily. To find out who they are and what they've done to date, letting the voter decide whether they have earned their vote. It's all about the person, not the party.

Let people decide for themselves based on data. You will be able to see what qualifications each candidate has for the position they seek, you can get a glimpse into their agenda's with their past voting history, what issues they've been involved in, and how active they are in the community they wish to represent. Furthermore, we want to make the government more accessible and more open to you, the voter. Never again will you be surprised by primary elections, or be affected by an obscure ordinance . everything you can act on, we promise to let you know when, where, and how you can make your voice heard. Representatives are just that, and now you can start making informed decisions about who is representing you. E Plurbus Unum - Out of many divided states, came one, utterly awesome country, based on the foundations of 'power to the people'. We stand stronger today, but no less divided. The principles which were the catalyst for change over 200 years ago, are again prowled to disrupt the political machine, and take back the people's voice. Find yours.

FAQ

What to you provide besides candidate info?
We plan to provide information on all votable initiatives in a user's area, including bills, local legislation, and zoning/city planning propositions.
Why should I use this?
Our only goal is to make all the information we can possibly find, available to you at no charge. The site is meerly a tool for letting you decide who to vote for.
